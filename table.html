Risk Response Matrix

Risk Mitigation Contingency
Weather and Environmental Risks Monitor weather forecasts and have contingency plans. Extend the expedition or provide alternative activities if needed.
Logistical Risks Partner with reliable transportation providers, maintain lost baggage protocol. Have backup transportation options, flexible schedules.
Health and Safety Risks Ensure guest awareness of safety guidelines and basic first aid training. Maintain a well-equipped first aid kit, establish communication protocol for emergencies.
Communication Risks Test communication devices before the expedition, provide backup communication methods. Develop a plan for non-functional devices, use guides as intermediaries.
Regulatory and Compliance Risks Stay updated on state regulations, ensure compliance. Have legal counsel available, ensure guests understand regulations.
